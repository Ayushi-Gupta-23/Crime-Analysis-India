Crime Against Women in India

Crime analysis against women in India is a critical area of concern that demands attention and action. Women's safety and security are essential for the overall well-being and progress of society. Analyzing and addressing crimes against women is pivotal in ensuring a safe and equitable environment for all.

Scope of the Issue:

  • Gender-Based Violence: This includes domestic violence, physical assault, sexual harassment, and emotional abuse, often perpetuated by individuals known to the victim.
  • Sexual Assault: Cases of rape and molestation highlight the alarming vulnerability of women, with far-reaching physical and psychological consequences.
  • Dowry-Related Crimes: Despite legal prohibitions, dowry-related harassment and violence persist in many parts of the country.
  • Human Trafficking: Women and girls are often victims of human trafficking, forced into various forms of exploitation, including forced labor and sexual slavery.
  • Honor Killings: Some parts of India still witness honor killings, where women are brutally murdered for perceived violations of family or community honor.
